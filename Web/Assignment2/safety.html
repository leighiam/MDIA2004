My Safety Tips on how to stay Covid-19 Free!




Stay Home


Unlike me, a lot of persons love being outside. They enjoy the thrill of being on the road with friends making memories that will last a lifetime. However, Covid-19 does not allow anyone to maintain their normal lifestyles, styaing home is a must if you want to combat the virus. If the streets can be avoided, stay home! Only leave the house for necessities and make sure to follow proper protocol when reentering your home to make sure you're not taking the virus with you.


Shower regurlarly


I'm assuming everyone bathes at least once everyday, well if not, now is the time. This is especially necessary after going on the road. This ensures that you wash away any particles you might have brought in with you, from your whole body, especially places you would not expect them to be.


Wash outside clothes immediately


As soon as you get home from outside, no matter how short of a time it was, be sure to wash the clothes you take off immediately. If not immediately, at least ensure that you store it away from anything else until it is time for laundry.


Keep your hands washed and sanitized


Always, always, always rememeber to wash your hands, after any and everything. After touching any surfaces, especially those outside of your home, please rememeber to sanitize your hands if you do not have access to a sink or soap to get your hands washed. Also rememeber to sanitize surfaces in your home, especially those frequently touched, for example door knobs.


Social Distancing


As an introvert, I always find myself at leats 8 feet apart from people especially in a line. Now the norm has been set to 6 feet which means I am 10 feet apart(*inserts laughter here*). Also stay away from your family members and friends whom you do not reside as way lessening the risk for everyone.


Wear your masks


Never, ever, ever, leave your house without a mask, especially since masks are mandatory in public spaces. If you can not afford to buy one, grab some material and make one, as long as it is sturdy enough to keep those particles away.
